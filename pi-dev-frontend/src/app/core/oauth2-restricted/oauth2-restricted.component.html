OAuth2 Account Access Limitation

Your account was authenticated through a third-party provider (Google or GitHub), but this area requires specific role permissions that are not assigned to your account.

This could be because:

  • Your OAuth2 account is not fully integrated with our role-based permission system
  • Your account needs additional authorization from an administrator
  • The requested resource is not available to external authentication users

To resolve this issue, you can:

  1. Contact an administrator to assign the necessary roles to your account
  2. Log out and sign in using a regular account with the appropriate permissions
